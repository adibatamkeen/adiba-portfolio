Projects

Healthcare Analytics
This project analyzes six months of data from Neelima Hospitals to improve patient care and operational efficiency, focusing on patient revisit rates as a key quality metric. It uses Python for data processing and Power BI and Tableau for visualization. The analysis covers inpatient, outpatient, and emergency departments, providing insights into resource allocation, patient satisfaction, and financial impacts. The results help stakeholders make informed decisions to enhance healthcare outcomes and optimize hospital performance.
Socialmedia Analysis
This project analyzes the "Nationalist Hub" YouTube channel’s performance using data from YouTube Studio and tools like Power BI and Python. It highlights strong engagement from loyal subscribers but notes declining interest among younger viewers and subscriber loss from the Watch Page. India leads in viewership, while the U.S. generates higher revenue. Recommendations focus on targeting younger audiences, improving retention, and boosting monetization to drive growth.
The impact of RBI on stock market indices
This project studies the impact of RBI’s monetary policies on Indian stock indices like Nifty50, Nifty Bank, and Nifty IT. Using exploratory data analysis and historical data, it examines how repo rate changes, CRR adjustments, and policy announcements affect market trends, liquidity, and investor sentiment. The findings reveal patterns linking RBI actions to stock market behavior, helping policymakers and investors understand market reactions to monetary policy decisions.
